FAQs

Anyone interested in Full Stack Web Development can join this program.

You can join even if you have no programming knowledge.

The content for this course is created by Technology Specialists, Full-Stack Developers and Engineering Managers from top tech companies like Amazon, IBM, Morgan Stanley, Uber Eats, OLX, Myntra and CarWale based on their experience of developing several successful products and on their experience of mentoring a team of Full-Stack Developers.

Thanks to their years of experience and domain knowledge, these professionals have instilled all the key concepts in an easily understandable format in this single course.

Based on their experience in Full-Stack Web Development, they have chosen the most important concepts that are fundamental to become a top Full-Stack Developer and crack interviews in top tech companies.

This program will follow a similar training structure as followed by top tech companies to train their Full-Stack Developers and will also cover important details from their interviews.

You will need 20 to 30 hours every week or 3-4 hours every day to do well in this course. This includes attending 6 to 8-hour live instructor-led classes every week and attending 6 to 8-hour doubt sessions every week.
